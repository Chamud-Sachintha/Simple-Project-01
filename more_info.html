About RoBoVine Dairy Product

A lightweight, ergonomic delivery system that automates the cleaning, disinfecting, stimulation, drying, and post-dip treatment of dairy cow teats. Consistent handling of the cow teat along with the ability to customize water temperature and air pressure reduces cow stress and increases milk flow-rate.

Eliminates the need for human contact with the cow teat and reduces the incidence of bacterial infection.

Lowers chemical residue in the milk by avoiding over-spray of disinfectant onto the udder and by more thoroughly washing and drying the teat. Reduces the use of chemical solution by two-thirds and eliminates the need for towels.
